F.A.Q

Quick Tips

What shoe repair services do you offer?

We provide complete footwear restoration services including shoe cleaning, polishing, sole replacement, heel repair, stitching repair, adhesive repair, leather restoration, recolouring, shoe stretching, mold removal, hardware replacement, and sock liner replacement.

Do you repair all types of shoes?

Yes. We repair leather shoes, sneakers, sports shoes, formal shoes, boots, sandals, heels, loafers, casual footwear, and premium branded shoes.

Can you repair damaged leather shoes?

Absolutely. Our leather repair services include crack repair, colour restoration, conditioning, scratch removal, polishing, and complete leather rejuvenation.

Do you offer sneaker cleaning services?

Yes. We provide professional sneaker cleaning, stain removal, deodorizing, whitening, sole cleaning, and complete sneaker restoration.

Can you replace worn-out shoe soles?

Yes. We offer both half-sole and full-sole replacement using durable, high-quality materials that improve comfort, grip, and longevity.

Do you repair broken or loose heels?

Yes. Our heel repair services include heel rebuilding, heel tip replacement, stabilisation, and complete heel restoration.

What is shoe stretching and how does it help?

Shoe stretching safely expands tight shoes to improve comfort and fit. It helps reduce foot pain, pressure points, blisters, and discomfort caused by tight footwear.

Can you stretch leather shoes without damaging them?

Yes. We use professional stretching equipment and techniques that maintain the shoe's shape, structure, and finish while improving fit.

Do you remove mold and mildew from shoes?

Yes. We offer specialized mold and mildew removal services to eliminate fungus, odors, moisture damage, and bacteria while restoring freshness and hygiene.

How do shoes develop mold and mildew?

Mold and mildew typically develop due to moisture, humidity, improper storage, or prolonged exposure to damp conditions.

Can moldy shoes be restored?

In most cases, yes. Our professional treatment removes mold, sanitizes the footwear, restores appearance, and helps prevent future fungal growth.

Do you provide shoe recolouring services?

Yes. We use premium dyes and colour-matching techniques to restore faded shoes and bring back their original appearance.

Can you fix shoes that are opening from the sole?

Yes. We provide industrial-strength adhesive repair services for shoes with detached soles, toe openings, or side separations.

Do you replace zippers, buckles, and hardware?

Yes. We replace damaged zippers, buckles, hooks, eyelets, rivets, and other shoe hardware using high-quality components.

What is a sock liner replacement?

A sock liner replacement involves replacing the inner sole or footbed to improve comfort, cushioning, hygiene, and support.
